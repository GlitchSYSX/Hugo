✦ The Story

WHO IS
HUGO?

Hugo is a blocky character with a whole lot of personality. Born in the streets of the metaverse, each Hugo carries its own vibe — from raging to royalty, from chill to completely shocked.

Created by Harold, this collection blends pixel-art culture with modern illustration to produce PFPs that actually say something about who you are.
